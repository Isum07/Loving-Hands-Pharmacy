About Us

We welcome you to find out more about the values and vision that guide us, the services we offer, the indicators that ensure our continued accountability and transparency, and the news that matter most to  you. At Loving Hands Healthcare, we believe that every patient deserves personalized attention and the best possible treatment options. Our multidisciplinary approach ensures that we collaborate closely with patients and their families to develop tailored care plans that meet their unique needs. From preventive care and emergency services to advanced surgeries and rehabilitation, we strive to support our patients at every stage of their healthcare journey. Thank you for choosing Loving Hands Healthcare as your trusted healthcare partner. We are here for you 24/7, dedicated to making a positive impact on your health and life.

Loving Hands Healthcare Central Nawala Loving Hands Healthcare Central Hospital is a comprehensive medical facility that provides a wide range of healthcare services to the community. With a 500-bed capacity, it offers emergency care, surgical services, maternity care, and various specialized departments such as cardiology, orthopedics, and neurology. The hospital is known for its state-of-the-art diagnostic equipment and a dedicated team of physicians, nurses, and support staff. It also features an attached outpatient clinic for routine check-ups and preventive care.
Loving Hands Healthcare Surgical Maradana Loving Hands Healthcare Surgical Hospital is a leading pediatric institution specializing in the care of infants, children, and adolescents. The hospital is equipped with child-friendly amenities and employs pediatric specialists in fields such as pediatric oncology, cardiology, and neurology. Its facilities include a pediatric intensive care unit (PICU), neonatal intensive care unit (NICU), and a play therapy area to help young patients feel more comfortable during their stay. The hospital also engages in research and educational programs focused on improving pediatric health outcomes
Loving Hands Healthcare Kandy Kandy Loving Hands Healthcare Kandy Hospital is a prestigious academic hospital affiliated with a major university. It serves as a teaching hospital and research facility, offering advanced medical care and participating in cutting-edge clinical trials. The hospital has specialized centers for complex diseases, including cancer, cardiovascular disorders, and rare genetic conditions. It is a hub for medical education, with residents and fellows working alongside leading experts in various medical fields. The center is also involved in research initiatives aimed at developing innovative treatments and improving healthcare practices
