Services

Loving Hands Healthcare is a premier healthcare hospital renowned for its extensive range of services, cutting-edge technology, and commitment to patient-centered care. As a leading medical center, Grandview combines advanced clinical care, research, and education to provide exceptional health services across a broad spectrum of medical specialties.


Emergency 24/7
Available around the clock, our ED handles a wide variety of urgent and life-threatening conditions with rapid assessment and treatment. Equipped with the latest diagnostic tools and staffed by highly trained emergency medicine physicians and nurses


Inpatient Care
Provides care for patients with a range of medical conditions, including chronic illnesses and post-surgical recovery. Features multidisciplinary teams that work together to create personalized treatment plans.


Surgical Services
A network of modern operating rooms equipped with advanced surgical technology, including robotic-assisted surgery, minimally invasive techniques, and traditional open surgery.


Diagnostic and Laboratory Services
Advanced imaging modalities, including MRI, CT scans, PET scans, and digital X-rays, providing precise diagnostic information to guide treatment decisions.


Outpatient Services
Comprehensive outpatient services in various specialties such as cardiology, oncology, endocrinology, gastroenterology, and more. Clinics are staffed by specialists who provide ongoing management and follow-up care.


Maternity and Newborn Services
offering a range of birthing options, including natural delivery, epidural anesthesia, and Cesarean sections. Dedicated support teams assist with prenatal care, childbirth,postnatal support
